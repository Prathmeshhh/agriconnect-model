Framework & Features


  • User Registration and Profiles:
    1. Farmers, industry professionals, and stakeholders can develop unique profiles with pertinent data.
    2. Users' legitimacy is ensured by a registration process that includes verification.

  • Dashboard and Navigation:
    1. UI with simple navigation and quick access to many options.
    2. A simple menu for navigation allows for easy browsing and exploration.

  • Information Hub:
    1. A thorough database of articles, manuals, tutorials, and research papers related to agriculture.
    2. Parts divided into categories for agri-inputs, market trends, animal management, and crop information.

  • Discussion Forums and Communities:
    1. Farmers can collaborate, seek advice, and share experiences in interactive discussion forums.
    2. To connect like-minded people, virtual communities are focused on geographic areas, crop varieties, or particular interests.

  • Market Connectivity:
    1. Farmers can advertise their goods online, set their own prices, and interact with potential customers via this platform.
    2. Integration of online payments to speed up secure transactions and monitor sales.

  • Government Support Integration:
    1. Integration with government databases to give data on grants, programmes, and subsidies is known as government support.
    2. Farmers can apply online to use government assistance programmes.

  • Customizable Tools and Applications:
    1. Interactive tools including crop planning calculators, modules for diagnosing pests and diseases, and frameworks for evaluating soil health.
    2. Dashboards for farm management that may be customized to track costs, stock, and output.
